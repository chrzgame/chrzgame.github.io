Retrogaming on the Cardano Blockchain
by @gamezonchain
What is CHRZ?
CHRZ is a retrogame that fully lives on the Cardano blockchain without the need of resorting to external hosting services (e.g., IPFS, arweave). Just 16kb of metadata: an impossible bottle, crafted with love and passion for coding, that includes animations, synthesized sound effects, touch and keyboard controls, and -last but not least- a lot of funny Unicode characters.
CHRZ anatomy

One HERO (you!) and four enemies. Each enemy has got his own personality.

  • SHADOWMAN aggressively chases HERO everywhere.
  • AMBUSHMAN tries to get ahead of HERO to ambush him.
  • THE PROFESSOR uses complex formulas to try to trap HERO. Nonetheless his strategy is often ineffective.
  • HAPPY STONER is not very hostile: when close enough to HERO it postpones his lethal goal. This guy is just pursuing happiness.

Two enemy modes

  • PATROL mode (duration: 8s): the enemies follow predefined paths.
  • CHASE mode (duration: 15s): the enemies behave according to their personality.
Game variants and rarity
There exists 1013 CHRZ tokens: 1000 tokens have been procedurally generated using a subset of the unicode emojis. The remaining 13 tokens are special 1/1 tokens where graphical elements (such as enemies, hero, power-ups) have been manually selected according to a specific game theme.
